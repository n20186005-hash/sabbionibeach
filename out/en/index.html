Overview

Spiaggia Sabbioni is one of the most popular and accessible beaches on Lake Garda, located in Riva del Garda in the Autonomous Province of Trento. It offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and crystal-clear waters.

  • Free pebble beach with spacious areas
  • Panoramic views of the Alps and Lake Garda
  • Ideal for families with children
  • Close to the historic centre of Riva del Garda
  • Restrooms and showers available
  • Perfect for swimming, windsurfing and sailing

Visitor Tips

Practical information to make the most of Spiaggia Sabbioni

1

Best Time to Visit

June to September for swimming. May and October offer fewer crowds and pleasant temperatures.

2

How to Get There

5-minute walk from Riva del Garda centre. Parking available nearby.

3

What to Bring

Water shoes (pebble beach), sunscreen, water. Umbrellas and sun loungers available for rent on-site.

4

Activities

Swimming, windsurfing, sailing, stand-up paddle. Lakeside path ideal for walks and cycling.

5

Safety

Generally calm waters, suitable for children. Watch for boats in areas further from shore.

6

Nearby

Visit Riva del Garda old town, La Rocca (civic museum), and Varone Waterfalls a few km away.
